Before exploring:


In the section below, you can explore poetry by Dalit authors, translated from Bengali, Hindi/Urdu, Marathi, and Tamil into English. These poems are accessible online and located in various anthologies, all linked by poem listed.

The poems listed under each poet are not representative of their entire body of work. Each box is color-coded according to the original langauge the poet wrote in: Bengali (pink), Hindi / Urdu (light brown), Marathi (no additional color), and Tamil (light teal).
